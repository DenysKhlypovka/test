1) To Add a company fill inputboxes in the "Add new Company" section. If the company to add is a main company, "Parent Company" field should be ignored. 
2) To Delete a company fill inputbox in the "Delete Company" section.
3) To Inspect a company fill inputbox in the "View Company" section. 
4) To Edit a company fill inputboxes in the "Edit Company" section. "Company Name" field is mandatory. "New Name", "Edit Estimated Earnings", 
"Change Parent Company" fields to be filled in order to change the corresponding values.

"Server response" section shows the status of the last request sent.
